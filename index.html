How to play:

  • Find the secret word by guessing a five-letter word. We have given you the first letter of the secret word.
  • Letters in green mean that your guessed word has a letter in the same position as the secret word.
  • Letters in yellow mean that your guessed word and the secret word share a letter, but it is not in the correct position.
  • Continue guessing until find the secret word.
